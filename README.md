Problem Statement - Personalized Learning Recommendation System using Deep Learning and LLM

What is the problem?
There are many courses on online learning platforms, but they are not truly personalized. They suggest content based on clicks or popularity rather than considering the learner’s needs and goals. Thus, they suggest generic recommendations not suitable for the learner’s level. They also do not provide learning path or topics in the correct order. Because of this, it is hard for the learner to pick the right content at the right time. 

Who does it affect? 

1. Students or Learners Beginners have no idea where to start. Intermediate learners get stuck because they lack the basics. Advanced learners spend their time reading irrelevant material. 

2. Professionals/Job Seekers They have trouble selecting the proper learning path to meet their career ambitions. They lack guidance with respect to skills progression like becoming a data scientist. 

3. Educators & EdTech Platforms They cannot provide personalized learning experiences at a large scale. This translates into reduced learner engagement and course completion rates. 

Why is it important?  
1. Low Learning Efficiency: Learners waste time on irrelevant or poorly sequenced content.  
2. Lack of Adaptive Learning: Most platforms do not adjust recommendations based on Performance and Understanding level.   
3. No Intelligent Guidance:Learners do not receive answers to:  
- What should I learn next?   
4. Cold Start Problem: New users get poor recommendations due to a lack of prior data.  
5. Need for Explainability:Users doubt recommendations because no explanation is provided.
